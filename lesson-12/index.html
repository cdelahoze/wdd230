Site Name

Curramba Hotel

Site Purpose

The website of the Curramba Hotel, a member of the Temple Inn & Suites hotel chain based in Bethesda, MD, aims to promote and provide information on the specialized services offered by this hotel chain to meet the needs of the clients of the Barranquilla Temple, Colombia, who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.
