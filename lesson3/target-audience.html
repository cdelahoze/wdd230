Description of the target audience

Traders from the northwestern region of the Department of Cundinamarca.

General public interested in starting a business.

Technical students, technologists and professionals who wish to provide their services through their own companies.

Personas


It is perceived that 70% of website users are between 20 and 50 years old, 10% would be students under 20 years old, and the remaining 20% ​​are over 50 years old. It is estimated that the proportion of men and women is equal.

Facataivá, being a rural area, most of the companies are agro-industrial. Additionally, according to Confecamaras, in Colombia, of the total number of registered and renewed companies, 87% are micro-enterprises (350,265), 9% small companies (38,151), 3% medium-sized (10,533), and 1% large companies (3,597). ). In this sense, it is estimated that 87% of the entrepreneurs who visit the website would be micro-enterprises, so the educational level would be between secondary education and university professionals. Depending on the type of business, stores, agricultural producers, hardware stores, and, to a lesser extent, professional services.

The work experience of the users would be between 2 and 20 years of service. The educational level of the professionals is mostly university professionals who provide medical, veterinary and agronomic, legal, and educational services.

People will go to the site to obtain information on the procedures to register their companies, carry out registration procedures, obtain registration and legal representation certifications, commercial registries, and access to virtual and face-to-face training in matters related to the activities of the Chamber of Commerce.

People will access the site from desktop computers, tablets, and cell phones. Therefore, the site will require allowing access from a browser and, if possible, developing a mobile application.
